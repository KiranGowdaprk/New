Our Story

Idas Idea was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to bring unique, creative, and high-quality products to customers who appreciate innovation and design. What started as a small boutique in Washington, DC has grown into a thriving e-commerce destination serving customers across the country.

Our name comes from the founder's grandmother, Ida, who always had a knack for finding the perfect item for any occasion. We carry on her legacy by curating a collection of products that combine functionality with exceptional design.

Today, we work with over 200 artisans and manufacturers to bring you products that you won't find anywhere else. Each item in our collection is carefully selected to ensure it meets our high standards for quality, design, and value.

Our Values

These principles guide everything we do at Idas Idea.

Quality

We never compromise on quality. Every product in our collection is thoroughly vetted to ensure it meets our high standards.

Innovation

We're always on the lookout for innovative products that solve problems in new and creative ways.

Customer Focus

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We're committed to providing exceptional service at every touchpoint.

Shipping Methods & Time

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days - $5.99
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days - $12.99
  • Overnight Shipping: 1 business day - $24.99

Free standard shipping is available on orders over $50.

Order Processing

Orders placed before 2 PM EST Monday-Friday are processed the same day. Orders placed after 2 PM EST or on weekends are processed the next business day.

International Shipping

We currently ship to the United States, Canada, and select European countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

Return Policy

  • Home
  • Return Policy

30-Day Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In the original packaging
  • Unused and in the same condition that you received it
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase

How to Return an Item

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team at returns@idasidea.com or call us at (202) 408-7904. We will provide you with a return authorization and shipping instructions.

Refunds

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed to your original method of payment within 5-10 business days.
